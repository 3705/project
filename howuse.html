How to Use - Creative Studio

1. Video Editing

Start by selecting the video you want to edit. Use our intuitive tools to trim, add effects, and enhance the audio. Preview your changes and save the final result.

2. Website Building

Create your website by selecting a template or starting from scratch. Customize the layout, add pages, and personalize the content. Preview your website and publish it to make it live.

3. CV Creation

Create a professional CV by filling in your details in the customizable templates. Highlight your skills, experience, and education. Download the CV in various formats.

4. Poster & Logo Design

Design eye-catching posters and logos using our customizable templates. Explore logo creation tools and export your designs in high-resolution formats for your projects.

5. Personal Card

Create a personal card to showcase your contact information and social links. Customize the design and share your card digitally or print it for in-person networking.
